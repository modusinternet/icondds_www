Family Dentistry

{CCMS_DB:all,homepage} / {CCMS_DB:all,services} / Family Dentistry

As the bedrock of society, family is the best and most important gift in our lives. From the very beginning our goal at ICONIC Dentistry is to create a trustworthy, caring, and supportive place where people can call it their dental home. Here, we look after your needs as a family member would.

Family dentistry addresses oral health at all ages and for all members of the family. Children have very different dental needs than adults, and our doctors have been trained to competently deliver care from baby (primary) teeth to adult (permanent) teeth.

A comprehensive family practice such as ICONIC Dentistry also emphasizes active prevention and early intervention. Have you heard the old saying, "an ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure"? While it’s great to catch a problem early and take care of it, it’s even better not to get it in the first place. This is especially important for children—a sugar-laden modern diet can silently inflict immense harm to their teeth—unless proper instructions are given at a young age and reinforced. The two most common, yet easily preventable afflictions that can result in premature tooth loss are caries and gum disease.

During regularly scheduled checkups and cleanings, we focus on prevention by educating patients of all ages on good oral hygiene practices. Whether they are teaching a toddler proper brushing techniques or showing an elderly patient the correct way to clean their dentures, our dentists, hygienists, and technicians make it a priority to see to it that you and your family members maintain good dental care habits.

Below are some of the services that represent active steps for prevention and early intervention.

  • Routine exams (including digital X-ray and non-radiation caries detection)
  • Prophy and perio maintenance
  • Remineralization of early caries
  • Ozone therapy (non-invasive anti-infective therapy)
  • Sealants (to seal deep grooves & fissures)

Once small cavities have formed and cannot be remineralized, we offer several restorative therapies based on the biomimetic concept to maximally conserve tooth structures and decrease the risk of pulpal injury so the tooth doesn’t need a root canal treatment in the future.

  • Resin infiltration treatment (no shot, no-drilling)
  • Composites (biocompatible, tooth-colored fillings)

We have several state-of-the-art laser units that our doctors use in conjunction with these procedures. At times these high-tech equipment can even help do away with the need for a painful shot and drilling.

  • Laser analgesia
  • Laser cavity filling (no-drilling)

At ICONIC, we do our best to ensure a positive office experience for your children, you, and the rest of your family in a caring and comfortable environment. Oftentimes, a child’s life-long fear of dentists was triggered from one stressful visit. Our doctors’ gentle approach helps children develop a positive association with regular dental appointments and help build good oral hygiene habits for life.

Instead of visiting multiple dentists, all your family’s dental needs can be met in one convenient location. We proudly serve patients in Tustin and surrounding cities in Orange County, California.
