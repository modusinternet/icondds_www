What We Believe

We believe that all people deserve living a dignified life and therefore should be treated with respect. That’s why at ICONIC we never treat our patients based on their insurance coverage or the size of their wallet. We will always keep your best interest in mind.

We also believe that people have the right and responsibility to choose what’s best for them. As such, we are committed to provide our patients with unbiased, complete and true information regarding their health, and will help them in making informed decisions. And, we promise never to pressure you into doing something you’re not comfortable with.

For a thorough explanation of what we believe in, go to Our Philosophy page.
