What We Believe

We believe that all people deserve living a dignified life and therefore should be treated with respect. That’s why at ICONIC we never treat our patients based on their insurance coverage or the size of their wallet. We will always keep your best interest in mind.

We also believe that you have the right and responsibility to choose what’s best for you. As such, we are committed to provide, to the best of our ability, thorough, unbiased and truthful information to our patients, and will help you to make informed decisions regarding your oral health. We promise never to pressure you into doing something you’re not comfortable doing.

To learn more about what we believe, go to Our Philosophy page.
